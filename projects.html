Gugulethu Community Hall

We have identified Gugulethu Community Hall as a facility that requires investigation in terms of optimising its use for other purposes. Given the dire need for job creation in the community, we propose that the facility be utilised for other social purposes and economic opportunities. A key component of optimising the facility will be to gain input from the surrounding community, and other users. The first part of optimising the facility is the scoping or information gathering phase. During this phase, we will analyse the facility and identify user needs as well as a project scope. Notice is hereby given in terms of Section 16(1) (a) of the Municipal Systems Act. In terms of section 17 of the Local Government: Municipal Systems Act, Act 32 of 2000, you can submit comments and recommendations to the municipality in respect of the proposed optimisation.
